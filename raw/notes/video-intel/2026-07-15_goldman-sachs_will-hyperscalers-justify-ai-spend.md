## TL;DR

The AI trade **bifurcated**: the *spenders* (hyperscalers) are being punished — multiples compressing, "underperforming massively" — while the *beneficiaries* (AI winners/semis) "took all the gains." Privorotsky's core mechanism is **reflexivity**: if the spender's stock de-rates while the receiver's keeps rising, eventually **the receiver de-rates too** — the split is an "unsustainable equilibrium." The market "needs to see an ROI **this quarter or next**" or the spend is "too big to sustain." Underneath, positioning is dangerous: **2x-levered-ETF AUM up ~50% YTD, half of it in semis/hardware**, huge call skew / almost no put skew. Q2 earnings (starting now) are the ROI test against a very high bar (~24% EPS growth, revisions that unusually went *up*). Europe is the diversifier that "works when the AI trade doesn't."
