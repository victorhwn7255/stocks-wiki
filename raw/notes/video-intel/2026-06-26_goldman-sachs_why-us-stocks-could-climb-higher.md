GS's Flood stays firmly **buy-the-dip / trend-higher**. The bull thesis rests on one linchpin — **earnings**: the Q1 median S&P stock grew earnings ~+14% ("best in decades"); Q2 median expectation is ~+9% YoY, which he expects to clear.
