## TL;DR

The video walks the **entire optical-interconnect chain bottom-up** — substrates (AXTI InP / Soitec SOI) → epitaxy (IQE) → laser chips (SIVE fabless / LITE-COHR-AAOI IDM) → foundries (WIN Semi for InP/GaAs, TSEM for silicon-photonics PIC) → DSP (AVGO/MRVL) → fiber (Corning) — and argues the CPO transition re-architects it: Goldman (per the video) sees optical interconnect TAM going **$15B → $154B by 2028, $91B of it CPO**, vs only **~$160M of actual CPO shipments in 2026**. The thesis is "grab the bottlenecks": value sits at the scarce upstream layers, not one winner-take-all platform. **This is, almost layer for layer, the vault's own photonics chokepoint family** — most of the video's names already have vault pages.
