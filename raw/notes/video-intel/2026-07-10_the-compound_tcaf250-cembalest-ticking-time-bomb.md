## TL;DR

Cembalest is a disciplined US-bull on the macro (dollar dominance intact; "China dumping Treasuries," gold-hoarding, and dollar-collapse narratives are debunked with data) — but the *real* ticking time bomb he names is **federal debt** (2031-32 crossover where ~100% of tax revenue services interest + entitlements; a ratings-downgrade threat ~3 years out). On markets, the vault-relevant core is that **he has turned cautious on the AI-infrastructure trade in the last three months**, using the **1999 Cisco→optical telecom playbook**: the hyperscaler *buyers* are "starting to roll over" while the semiconductor index zooms, hyperscaler capex funding has **flipped from internal cash flow to borrowing** (FCF projections "heading very low"), **custom ASICs are eroding NVDA's share** (JPM: 30-40% TCO edge → NVDA "stuck in the mud" at ~18-20× forward), and **free Chinese open models now take ~50% of US-company tokens**. The whole thing rests on an **~18-month ROI test**: hyperscalers must prove enterprises are adopting and *paying* for AI (not routing to free Chinese models) or the ~$1.5T of infra capex un-earns its return.
