## Contradictions / red-flags

Large-cap, real sell-side coverage → **low pump risk, substantive flow.** Watch items are about *interpretation*, not fabrication:

1. **★ Feature-count inflation ("100+" vs "170+").** Datadog's own press release says "**more than 100**" new features; one blog roundup says "**more than 170**." Marketing counts conflate GA, preview, and re-packaged capabilities — treat the number as **IR-tier framing, not substance**. `[verify:]` how many DASH launches are actually GA + revenue-attached at the Q2 call.
2. **★ The AI-cohort revenue-level gap persists — and Bernstein put a number on it.** The vault's Open Q#1 is that Datadog discloses the AI-native cohort's *growth contribution* (~7pts) but never its *revenue level*. Bernstein now estimates **non-AI ≈ 85% of revenue** (→ AI-native ≈ ~15%) — a **Tier-4 sell-side estimate, NOT a company disclosure.** It's a useful triangulation of the concentration magnitude but must **not** be cited as fact; the clean line still only comes from a filing.
3. **★ Bull/bear split is unusually wide.** Bernstein's $226 sits *below* the ~$248 print while UBS/Benchmark are at $315/$330 — the disagreement is about **timing** (can non-AI growth sustain the priced-in expectations into Q3/Q4), not franchise quality. This is exactly the vault's consumption-dial tension made explicit.
4. **Hot tape into a hard print.** +70% in a month, golden cross, and ~$176M insider selling (all T5) raise **expectations risk** into the Aug 6 Q2 report — a beat may be needed just to hold. Momentum ≠ thesis change; do not weight the price action.
5. **Aligned-incentive alert on DASH coverage.** Most DASH product detail traces to Datadog's own blog/PR + partner/press amplification (SiliconANGLE, StockTitan) — **Tier-2 management marketing.** The products are real; the "autonomous ops / agentic" framing is promotional. Confirm materiality (adoption, monetization) at the primary, not the keynote.
6. **Gartner Leader = credible but conflicted.** 6th-year MQ Leader is a genuine competitive marker, but analyst-firm placements are **Tier-3/4** and Datadog is paying-adjacent to the coverage ecosystem — supportive, not dispositive.
