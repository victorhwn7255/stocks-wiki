## Segment structure: FAS + FRS

**Flex Agility Solutions ("FAS")** — optimized for speed to market with highly flexible supply and manufacturing. Three end markets:
- **Communications, Enterprise and Cloud ("CEC")** — data center, edge, communications infrastructure. **JetCool included in CEC reporting unit per FY2025 10-K Note 19.** Q3 FY2026: high single-digit % CEC growth driven by data center cloud demand. 9M FY2026: mid-teen % CEC growth from data center cloud.
- **Lifestyle** — appliances, floorcare, smart living, HVAC, power tools. Q3 FY2026 mid single-digit % growth from Orangeburg manufacturing facility (acquired February 4, 2025).
- **Consumer Devices** — mobile and high velocity consumer devices. Q3 FY2026 mid single-digit % decline; 9M high-teen % decline.

**Flex Reliability Solutions ("FRS")** — optimized for longer product lifecycles requiring complex ramps with specialized production models and critical environments. Three end markets:
- **Industrial** — industrial devices, capital equipment, renewables, **critical power, embedded power**. Q3 FY2026 high-teen % growth "primarily in power due to data center growth and business acquisitions" (Crown contribution + organic). Anord Mardix critical power capabilities operate within FRS Industrial.
- **Automotive**
- **Health Solutions** — Q3 FY2026 strong demand growth (medical devices)
