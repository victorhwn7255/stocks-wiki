## Product portfolio + PowiGaN technology

### High-voltage power conversion IC families

POWI's core product portfolio across multiple IC families addressing AC-DC + DC-DC + gate driver + motor driver applications:
- **InnoSwitch™** — Off-line CV/CC switcher ICs with integrated PowiGaN GaN transistors (7 mentions at 10-K)
- **LinkSwitch™** — Low-power off-line switcher ICs (2 mentions)
- **BridgeSwitch™** — Motor-driver ICs (eliminate heatsinks in many applications per 10-K)
- **EcoSmart™** — Energy efficiency technology across product portfolio
- **PI Expert™** — Online design software tool

Product power range: AC-DC supplies up to ~500 watts; high-voltage gate drivers serve applications from ~100 kilowatts to gigawatts (industrial motor drives + HVDC transmission + EV powertrain scope per 10-K Item 1).

### PowiGaN GaN technology positioning (3 mentions at 10-K)

PowiGaN = proprietary GaN transistor technology integrated within InnoSwitch family. Per 10-K Item 1: "proprietary GaN transistor technology offers substantially higher levels of active-mode efficiency compared to traditional silicon switches." Q1 2026 earnings press release CEO framing positions PowiGaN at "EVs and AI data centers" applications with "DC transmission" + gate driver scope. 49 GaN mentions total across 10-K (PowiGaN-specific scope smaller).
