## Product portfolio + technology positioning

### GaNFast™ GaN power ICs (primary product family — 89 GaN mentions at 10-K)

Integrated gallium nitride power ICs combining GaN power FET + driver + control + sensing + protection. Targeted at AI data center 800V DC power delivery (specifically 800V→6V or 800V→50V conversion stages per Q1 2026 earnings disclosure), high-performance computing, fast charging, motor drive applications. 30+ years combined wide bandgap expertise per Navitas positioning.

### GeneSiC™ high-voltage SiC MOSFETs (secondary product family — 61 SiC mentions at 10-K)

Patented trench-assisted planar SiC technology. 5th-generation 1200V SiC MOSFET portfolio expanded Q1 2026 with top-side-cooled QDPAK + low-profile TO-247-4L packages targeted at AI Data Center PSU applications. Higher-voltage variants (3300V) demonstrated at APEC 2026 in 250kW solid-state transformer partnership with EPFL.

### Dual-technology AI data center positioning

Management frames GaN + SiC as complementary in AI power architecture: "GaN and high-voltage SiC are playing equally vital roles in the AI power revolution, and Navitas is uniquely positioned with both technologies enabling more content, broader applications and a larger portion of the growth opportunity" (Allexandre CEO, Q1 2026 earnings). Distinct from single-technology power-semi peers (Wolfspeed pure-SiC; Power Integrations primarily silicon + GaN niches).

### Patent portfolio + manufacturing model

10-K Item 1: 300+ patents issued or pending; CarbonNeutral®-certified (first semiconductor company). Fabless model with foundry partners: GlobalFoundries (GaN; US-based) + X-Fab (SiC; US-based with Texas operations). 10-K Risk Factors frames US-based foundry partners as competitive advantage for AI data center customers requiring supply chain transparency.
