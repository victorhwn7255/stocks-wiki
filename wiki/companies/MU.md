## HBM positioning

**HBM3E generation:** Q4 FY2025 HBM3E 12-high represented majority of HBM shipments per FY2025 10-K. Enhanced version delivers faster data rates, improved thermal response, higher monolithic die density within same package footprint.

**HBM4 36GB 12-high:** Volume shipments started Q1 calendar 2026 — **designed for NVIDIA Vera Rubin** (per Q2 FY2026 prepared remarks; NVIDIA named at Tier 2 earnings deliverable). FY2025 10-K Tier 1 disclosure: "delivered samples of HBM4 36GB 12-high to multiple key customers to power next-generation AI platforms" (customer NOT named at Tier 1 SEC filing). Production ramp + volume shipments underway; expected to reach mature yields faster than HBM3E.

**HBM4 16-high:** Sampled Q2 FY2026 — 48GB per HBM cube (33% capacity increase vs HBM4 12H).

**HBM4E next-generation:** Volume ramp expected calendar 2027 on 1γ DRAM technology node. Customization options for AI compute platform differentiation per Mehrotra Q2 FY2026 prepared remarks.

**Technology nodes:**
- 1γ (1-gamma) DRAM — highest-volume node in MU history; fastest ramp to mature yields; on track for majority of DRAM bit mix by mid-calendar 2026
- 1δ (1-delta) DRAM — increased EUV adoption planned with latest-generation EUV tools
- G9 NAND — on track for majority of bits by mid-calendar 2026; record QLC (quad-level cell) bits mix Q2 FY2026

**LP DRAM for data center (Micron-pioneered):** 256GB LP SOCAMM2 product (1γ node) enabling 2TB per CPU — quadrupled content from prior year. **NVIDIA Groq 3 LPX** (GTC March 2026 announcement) implements up to 12TB DDR5 in rack scale architecture (NVIDIA named at Tier 2 prepared remarks).

**Manufacturing footprint expansion:**
- **Tongluo (Taiwan) site:** Acquisition from Powerchip Semiconductor closed Q2 FY2026 — meaningful product shipments from existing fab fiscal 2028; plan to begin construction of second cleanroom by end FY2026
- **Idaho fab 1:** initial wafer output mid-calendar 2027
- **Idaho fab 2:** ground preparation started
- **New York fab:** ground broken; preparation activities ahead of plan
- **Hiroshima (Japan) site:** cleanroom expansion progressing
- **Singapore NAND fab:** ground broken; initial wafer output 2H calendar 2028
- **India assembly/test facility:** commercial shipments commenced — among largest single-floor assembly + test cleanrooms globally
- **Singapore advanced packaging facility for HBM:** meaningful contribution to HBM supply calendar 2027
