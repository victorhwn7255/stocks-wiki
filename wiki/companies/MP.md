## Defense & Drones thesis role (dual-thesis)

MP is the vault's **first dual-thesis page** under the two-domain scope (CLAUDE.md v9.4 Section 1.2): one company sitting in both the AI-datacenter supply chain (above; `materials_tier 1`) and the Defense & Drones supply chain. Defense placement is **`defense_tier 4`** (supply-chain / materials enabler) per `frameworks-defense-drones.md` Framework D2 value-capture tiers. Per the codified `defense_tier` semantics, Tier 4 is the *highest-conviction* defense tier, not the lowest — it is where the chokepoint owners sit (see CLAUDE.md Section 3.2 convention note).

**The fit is DIRECT on the defense side — stronger than the AI side.** The AI-datacenter framing above is honest that MP's AI fit is *indirect* (rare-earth magnets sit 2-3 layers upstream of AI compute demand; cooling-motor NdFeB is a derivative application). The defense framing inverts that: MP is a **direct, highest-conviction chokepoint owner.** The transformational deal of the company — the U.S. Department of War public-private partnership (see Pentagon partnership scope, below) — *is* a defense-supply-chain deal. The defense thesis is where MP's structural position is most central, not most peripheral.

**Chokepoint quality gradient placement (Framework D5 chokepoint framework): the #1 geology/physics chokepoint.** Rare-earth NdFeB magnets are the most concentrated, most durable, best-funded chokepoint in the Defense & Drones thesis. Durability rests on geology + refining/separation capacity rather than policy, so it ranks above policy-created chokepoints such as the NDAA-compliant "Blue UAS" supply chain (which carries a known expiry — the DoD exemption ending January 1, 2027). Concentration scope (Tier-3 per the anchor report, citing IEA Nov 2025): China ~85-90% of finished magnets, ~91% of separation/refining, ~69% of mining, ~100% of production equipment; one Chinese firm (JL MAG) ≈ all non-Chinese output combined. The Pentagon's stated need is ~3,000-4,000 tons of specialized magnets annually, rising toward ~10,000 tons by 2030, against minimal current US output — the structural gap MP's Independence + 10X build-out targets.

**Drones are a small-volume but high-criticality consumer (honest scoping).** Drones are <3% of global NdFeB demand (Tier-3 per the report), so MP's defense relevance is the broad rare-earth reshoring it anchors, not drone-specific volume. The drone angle's weight is *criticality*, not tonnage: unmanned-system motors skew toward the most China-controlled heavy rare earths (dysprosium / terbium) for high-temperature performance — which ties directly to MP's heavy-rare-earth (Dy/Tb) separation commissioning at Mountain Pass (see Production positioning) and the Samarium Project Loan scope. No drone-demand overclaim: the offtake + price-floor economics are not drone-demand-driven.

**The DoW deal as chokepoint template + the budget-contingency discipline.** The Series A Cumulative Perpetual Convertible Preferred Stock ($400M / $413.6M net; 13.32M-share conversion at $30.03) + 10-year NdPr price floor + 10-year magnet offtake + Samarium Project Loan is the clearest case in either vault domain of the government converting a chokepoint into a backstopped commercial position (DPA Title III authority). It is also the defense thesis's **enacted-vs-requested budget discipline in action**: the page's own Pentagon partnership scope quotes the 10-K Item 1A risk that DoW funding is partly *"contingent on future appropriations"* — the same contingency the Defense thesis flags for the requested FY2027 DAWG funding. Government-as-capital-provider is a tracked Framework D3 (demand-map / programs-of-record) pattern — compare the reported Office of Strategic Capital equity talks with drone makers; MP is the most-developed instance.

**Cross-thesis node.** Rare-earth NdFeB magnets are the cleanest *multi-thesis chokepoint*: the same China concentration constrains defense/drone motors, AI-datacenter power-semiconductor + cooling-motor magnets, and EV/wind. One chokepoint, multiple demand drivers — MP is the shared node. Defense anchors: `_thesis-defense-drones.md` + `frameworks-defense-drones.md`. The execution-risk + bilateral-geopolitical-tail-risk framing in the Thesis role above applies identically under the defense lens (high-conviction strategic position bounded by thin operating history + appropriations dependence).
