## FLNC commercial structure (three product lines)

FY2025 10-K Item 1 frames FLNC offerings as three discrete product lines per Section 3.3 paraphrase:

1. **Energy storage solutions** — battery-based BESS hardware with integrated controls + monitoring + Fluence OS proprietary energy management system; sold to utilities + IPPs + developers + commercial-and-industrial customers
2. **Services** — recurring operational and maintenance (O&M) services with 24/7 real-time monitoring; service plans range from training-only to comprehensive performance guarantees + system testing + equipment warranties; long-term service agreements (LTSAs) provide multi-year revenue visibility
3. **Digital applications and software** — Fluence Mosaic (intelligent forecasting + bid optimization SaaS; available NEM Australia + CAISO + ERCOT + Japan) + Fluence Nispera (AI-driven asset performance management platform supporting energy storage + solar + wind portfolios)

Three-product-line discrete commercial structure per FY2025 10-K Item 1 — NOT integrated-deployment ("BESS-as-a-service") framing per Caveat #9 Trigger #3.

## Product family

**Gridstack Pro®** — large-scale front-of-the-meter applications; 2- and 4-hour configurations; balanced power-to-energy matching + increased site density; Gridstack Pro 2000 series utilizes Fluence-designed Battery Packs (Utah contract manufacturer; Tennessee cells for domestic content IRA/OBBBA eligibility).

**Gridstack®** — front-of-the-meter applications for IPPs + utilities + generators; designed for flexible peaking capacity + frequency regulation + renewable integration in regulated + deregulated electricity markets globally.

**Ultrastack®** — distribution and transmission network applications; stringent uptime + IT security requirements; advanced controls applications for highly technical grid services (synthetic inertia + power oscillation damping).

**Smartstack™** — announced FY2025 split-architecture design; embedded intelligence + higher energy density vs traditional AC systems; separates critical control systems from battery packs; edge computing + sensor network for real-time insights. **Data center application differentiator** per Q2 FY2026 call commentary — SmartStack density positioned for longer-duration energy storage in data center applications.

**Fluence OS** — proprietary energy management system included with all storage solutions; enables grid services (frequency regulation + secondary frequency response + fast frequency response + peak shaving + voltage regulation + capacity peak power + solar time-shifting + firm solar export).

## Software stack moat

**Mosaic** — intelligent energy forecasting + bid optimization software-and-service offering for utility-scale storage and renewable assets; enables customers to optimize asset trading in wholesale electricity markets; available NEM (Australia) + CAISO (California) + ERCOT (Texas) + Japan.

**Nispera** — AI-driven asset performance management (APM) platform supporting portfolios of energy storage + solar + wind assets; machine learning + AI tools for predictive failure detection + productivity improvement + TCO reduction.

Software stack proprietary moat positioning distinct from hardware-only BESS competitors (Tesla Megapack vertical integration; BYD cost-competitive; Wartsila hybrid power; ABB; Sungrow; Honeywell). Q2 FY2026 call substantiates competitive differentiation framing — selection processes for both new hyperscaler MSAs began with as many as 26 vendors; Fluence cited "understanding of customer requirements, rapid response time, and differentiated products" as winning factors (per Nebreda Q2 FY2026 call).
