## Segment structure

Eaton operates four primary reportable segments (FY2025 reporting structure):

**Electrical Americas** — power distribution, power quality, switchgear, transformers, busbar, modular solutions, electrical control products. Largest segment by revenue. Data center concentration dominant. Fibrebond integration (acquired April 2025) within this segment. Q4 2025 organic growth +15% with data center growth +40%.

**Electrical Global** — international power distribution, switchgear, transformers, control products. Q4 2025 +6% organic growth with "exceptional strength in data center demand" (ETN Q4 2025 call). Resilient Power Systems acquisition integrated into this segment.

**Aerospace** — aerospace fluid power, fuel systems, thermal management, motion control. Long-term service agreement (LTSA) attached. Ultra PCS Limited (closed January 23, 2026) integration. Q4 2025 +12% organic growth; 24.1% margin.

**Vehicle + eMobility** — vehicle drivetrain components + electric vehicle power management. **Mobility spinoff announced** at FY2025 earnings call (Feb 3, 2026); expected effective Q2 2026; ~$3B revenue carve-out preserves Electrical+Aerospace pure-play exposure post-spinoff. Strategic continuation of portfolio transformation pattern (Lighting 2020; Hydraulics 2021; Mobility 2026).
